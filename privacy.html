Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Flow AI ("Flow AI", "we", "our", or "us").

Flow AI is a personal finance and expense-tracking application that allows users to record expenses, scan receipts using artificial intelligence, organize purchases into categories, and better understand their spending.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the Flow AI mobile application, website, and related services.

By using Flow AI, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

  • Merchant names
  • Purchase amounts

  • Expense categories
  • Receipt photos submitted for analysis
  • Manually entered transaction information

  • Custom categories
  • Feedback and support requests

If you sign in using Apple Sign In, Google Sign In, or email authentication, we may receive basic account information necessary to create, authenticate, and manage your account.

The information received may include your name, email address, account identifier, and profile information, depending on the authentication method you choose and your account settings.

Receipt Photos

Flow AI allows users to upload or capture receipt images for expense analysis.

Receipt photos may be processed to:

  • Identify the merchant
  • Extract the purchase amount
  • Extract the purchase date
  • Identify purchased items, when supported
  • Suggest an expense category
  • Create an expense record
  • Improve the user experience

Receipts may contain personal or sensitive information that appears in the image. You should review receipts before uploading them and avoid submitting images containing information that is not necessary for expense tracking.

Receipt photos and extracted information are not sold to third parties.

Financial Information

Flow AI may store information about expenses that you add manually or extract from receipts, including:

  • Merchant names
  • Transaction amounts
  • Purchase dates
  • Expense categories
  • Currency
  • Notes associated with an expense
  • Spending history and summaries

Flow AI is an expense-tracking tool and is not a bank, payment provider, financial institution, accounting service, or financial adviser.

Flow AI does not require access to your bank account unless such functionality is introduced and clearly disclosed in the future.

Artificial Intelligence Processing

Flow AI uses artificial intelligence services, including OpenAI, to analyze receipt images and extract expense-related information.

Information submitted for analysis may be processed by third-party artificial intelligence providers solely for the purpose of providing the requested functionality.

Information sent for artificial intelligence processing may include:

  • Receipt images
  • Merchant information

  • Purchase dates
  • Text visible on receipts
  • Expense categories
  • Other information necessary to process the receipt

AI-generated results may not always be accurate. Users should review extracted information before saving or relying on it.

Flow AI does not provide financial, tax, legal, accounting, or investment advice.
