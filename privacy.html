Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Cally ("Cally", "we", "our", or "us").

Cally is a nutrition and calorie-tracking application that allows users to estimate calories and nutritional information from food photos using artificial intelligence.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the Cally mobile application, website, and related services.

By using Cally, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

  • Name

  • Profile information
  • Weight and body measurements
  • Nutrition goals
  • Food photos submitted for analysis
  • Feedback and support requests

Information Collected Automatically

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type

  • App version
  • IP address
  • General location information
  • Usage statistics

If you sign in using Apple Sign In, Google Sign In, or Email Authentication, we may receive basic account information necessary to create and manage your account.

Food Photos

Cally allows users to upload food images for nutritional analysis.

Food photos are processed to:

  • Identify food items
  • Estimate calories
  • Estimate protein, carbohydrates, and fat
  • Improve user experience

Food photos are not sold to third parties.
