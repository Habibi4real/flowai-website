Delete Your Flow AI Account
How to delete your account

You can request deletion of your Flow AI account and associated data at any time.

The fastest way is inside the Flow AI app: open Profile, go to Settings, tap Delete account, and confirm your request.

If you cannot access the app, email us at flowaipp@gmail.com from the email address connected to your Flow AI account. Use the subject line Delete my Flow AI account.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, Flow AI deletes or anonymizes data associated with your account, including:

Your account profile and authentication association
Your onboarding answers and financial preferences
Your saved expense categories and preferred currency
Your expense history, receipt scans, uploaded receipt photos, and extracted expense data
Your push notification token and app preference data
What may be kept

Some information may be retained when required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, tax, accounting, dispute resolution, or payment records.

Subscription and payment records are handled by Google Play or Apple App Store according to their own policies. Deleting your Flow AI account does not automatically erase records that those stores are required to keep.

Backup copies and server logs may take up to 90 days to be fully removed from our backup systems, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Processing time

We aim to process valid account deletion requests within 30 days. We may contact you if we need to verify account ownership before deleting the account.

Need help?

For questions about account deletion or personal data, contact Flow AI at flowaipp@gmail.com.
